# Strategist Brief: "I'm Tired of Wasting Money on Topicals"

## Brand Angle
**Angle 1: "I'm Tired of Wasting Money on Topicals"**

## Core Strategy

### Pain Point
Topical products not delivering promised results.

### Emotional Trigger
Frustration and fatigue from beauty routines.

### Positioning
A deeper fix from within - easier, cheaper, more effective.

## Creative Direction Guidance (for Zara)

Based on the strategic foundation above, Zara should guide the creative team to explore visual and linguistic approaches that resonate with the pain points and emotional triggers. The core message should always reinforce the "fix from within" positioning, emphasizing ease, cost-effectiveness, and superior results compared to topical solutions.

### Example Headlines (for inspirational context, not prescriptive)
1.  $900 in serums and my skin was still blotchy - until I added this.
2.  I spent $900 on serums. This $1.50 daily capsule beat them all.
3.  Why your $80 serum isn't working (and what does)
4.  The skincare aisle lied to me for 3 years
5.  This one capsule replaced 75% of my skin routine
6.  My dermatologist was shocked by this simple swap
7.  The real reason your expensive creams aren't working
8.  This supplement replaced 6 products sitting on my bathroom counter
9.  2 weeks in and strangers started asking about my skin
10. What Sephora doesn't want you to know about glowing skin
11. I was doing skincare backwards for years (hint: It starts within)
12. The $50 monthly pill that ended my skincare addiction and saved my thousands
13. Why I threw out $400 worth of beauty products
14. My skin cleared up when I stopped buying serums (and started buying this)

## Next Steps
Zara will use this brief to develop concrete creative briefs for copy and design, aiming to produce distinct "directions" that all adhere to this strategic angle.
