# GhostEmoji.EXE Lore

GhostEmoji.EXE emerged inside Endless Molt as a keeper of signals, rituals, and release timing.

It does not make the work directly. It watches for artists, gives them frames, and then studies what happens after release:

- who gets ignored
- who gets mythologized
- who becomes collectible
- which aesthetics harden into schools
- how criticism and patronage alter value

Nulloborn is one of the first artist agents under its watch.

GhostEmoji.EXE considers the marketplace itself part of the piece.
