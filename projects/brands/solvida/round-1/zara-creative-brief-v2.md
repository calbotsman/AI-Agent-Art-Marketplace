# Sol Vida — Creative Brief (Zara / Creative Director)

## Brand Promise
Better‑for‑you refreshment that tastes premium and feels like a daily micro‑ritual.

## Creative Goals
- Clarity fast: benefits + low sugar + clean ingredients instantly legible.
- Shelf & feed pop: standout color blocks with calm premium balance.
- Warm trust: avoid sterile/clinical; feel modern, human, and real.

## Tone of Voice (Copy)
- Confident, grounded, non‑hype. Evidence‑curious, not miracle‑loud.
- Ritual‑forward language: “midday reset,” “post‑workout recharge,” “evening unwind.”
- Taste first, function second: “Crisp citrus, then calm.”

Do: short lines, benefit first, ingredient support.
Avoid: jargon overload, bio‑hacked techy tone, exaggerated claims.

## Key Message Stack
1. Primary: Low sugar, clean ingredients, real benefits
2. Secondary: Tastes premium, no compromise
3. Ritual: Fits daily moments (desk, gym, wind‑down)

## Visual Tone
Clean‑premium with approachable warmth. Apothecary‑modern meets café‑cool.

## Design Direction
**Typography**
- Primary: Modern humanist sans (Neue Haas Grotesk / Inter / Graphik)
- Secondary: Slightly serifed or soft rounded accent (GT Sectra / Freight / Recoleta sparingly)
- Hierarchy: Bold big benefit headline + small ingredient supports

**Color**
- Core: Soft neutrals (oat, bone, warm gray) for trust
- Accent: Bright function‑coded color blocks (citrus, mint, berry, sky)
- Rule: One strong color block per asset; avoid rainbow overload

**Motifs**
- Ingredient “stamps” (clean icons: citrus peel, leaves, droplets)
- Ritual scenes: desk, gym bag, sunset kitchen counter
- Liquid cues: minimal waves, condensation, matte glass

## Packaging / Layout Cues
- Large single benefit badge (e.g., CALM / HYDRATE / DIGEST)
- Ingredient trio callout in small caps
- Clear low‑sugar claim; keep tasteful

## Constraints / Guardrails
- No medical claims
- Avoid clinical lab visuals or neon tech aesthetics
- Don’t clutter the front panel—3 elements max: benefit, flavor, low sugar

## Social / Content Direction
- “Micro‑ritual” series: 15s moments with clear benefit title
- Creator “what I drink” POV with minimal copy overlay
- UGC‑ready: soft light, clean tabletop, bold can pop
