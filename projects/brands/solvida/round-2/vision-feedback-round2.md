# Sol Vida — Vision Feedback (Round 2 renders)

## Comp A (Balanced Split)
- Competing focal points: huge headline + bright orange block + large product photo all demand attention. Reduce headline size/contrast or tone down background so one element is primary.
- Secondary info hierarchy is weak: badges/labels have similar weight to product name/supporting copy; product name and instructions are low.

## Comp B (Centered Stack)
- Headline dominates, but brand and supporting lines lose hierarchy; “Sol Vida” too small for brand recognition and subhead competes with headline. Increase brand or move it closer; reduce subhead weight/scale.
- Small, thin copy over textured orange background hurts legibility; increase contrast or move microcopy to calmer field.

## Comp C (Diagonal Split)
- Two competing focal points: top headline and large “Sol Vida” wordmark split attention. Reduce headline weight or scale down wordmark to clarify primary message.
