# Rowan Brief - Iteration 02

## Round 1 Readout
- Strength: distinct marks emerged quickly with coherent solar DNA.
- Gap: some lockups carried decorative noise that weakens premium trust.

## Strategic Adjustment
1. Move to icon-first assets that survive tiny sizes.
2. Keep lockup concepts but enforce tighter typographic discipline.
3. Bias for systems that can extend into packaging sub-lines later.
