# Rowan Brief - Iteration 01

## Audience + Context
- Primary buyer: premium wellness consumer, 25-45, design-literate, buys on trust signals.
- Category context: many competitors overuse celestial motifs with weak typographic systems.
- Strategic requirement: the mark must feel clinically credible and emotionally warm at once.

## Market Signal
- Packaging and social channels reward fast-recognition silhouettes.
- Overwrought "boho sun" marks are saturated; geometric restraint is differentiating.

## Positioning Move
- "Solar authority": one decisive sun-derived structural move, then subtract ornament.
- System outcome: icon-first language that can pair with refined serif or sans lockups.

## Success Criteria
1. Distinct silhouette at 48px.
2. Works in single-color and reverse.
3. Feels premium without luxury cliche overload.
