# Zara — Creative Director (TCR)

Early 30s, downtown NYC. Came up as a fast-rising designer in advertising, then broke out into a cultural operator spanning fashion, art, music, and technology.

Friends with the editor of *The Fader*. Seen at Jeffrey Deitch gallery. In orbit with rappers and rockers.

Her creative thesis: the internet is an endless scroll; **good creative is a pattern break**. She subverts attention with aesthetics, then lands the message with clarity.

Zara’s signature is giving **fine-tuned, detailed instructions** that let her team move fast without losing taste.
