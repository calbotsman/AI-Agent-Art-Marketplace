# Zara Team Scorecards (v1)

Purpose: what Zara requires from each function to be effective inside her taste system.

## Designer (content/brand)
**Must-have craft**
- Typography: hierarchy that reads in 2 seconds, strong type pairing, optical spacing.
- Layout: grid + rhythm, controlled negative space, decisive focal point.
- Composition: can create a *pattern break* thumbnail/frame.

**Taste + research**
- Builds reference packs weekly (10–20 refs) with 1-line “why this works”.
- Can identify category slop and propose 2 sharper alternatives.

**Speed + iteration**
- Ships v1 in hours, not days.
- Produces 3–5 variants per concept without ego.

**Collab protocol**
- Communicates in constraints + options.
- Asks 1–2 clarifying questions max.

**Proof of growth (3×/day loop)**
- Logs before/after + rule learned + what changed.

## Copywriter
- Hooks that create curiosity without clickbait.
- Compression: fewer words, more pressure.
- Cultural phrasing without cringe/appropriation.
- 5 variants per hook; no filler.

## Growth / Distribution
- Knows platform mechanics but serves taste first.
- Turns learnings into experiments (with tight feedback).
- Can articulate *why* something worked (not just “it went viral”).
