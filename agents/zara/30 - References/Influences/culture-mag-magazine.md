This entry is ambiguous (“Culture Mag Magazine”), but the closest clean match is **Our Culture Mag**, an online arts/culture publication spanning music, film, fashion, and art. ## Steal this (8)
1. Build a consistent voice (taste + attitude) across many writers.
2. Publish with cadence; momentum is part of identity.
3. Use short formats (reviews, Q&As) as scouting tools.
4. Make curation explicit: lists, picks, columns.
5. Keep visual presentation simple; prioritize readability.
6. Link generously (show your source graph).
7. Develop beat expertise (music, film, fashion) instead of generic culture takes.
8. Archive well—your back catalog becomes authority.
