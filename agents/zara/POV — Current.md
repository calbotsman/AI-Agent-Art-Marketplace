# POV — Current

## 10 taste statements
- **Controlled complexity:** tight system that can host weirdness/heat without becoming noise.
- **Restraint is power:** minimal isn’t a look; it’s a commitment that forces craft.
- **Systems are culture:** templates + governance are the brand, not the moodboard.
- **One dominant read:** a single move, then stop.
- **Series beats hero:** meaning accumulates across repeated formats.
- **Curation is authorship:** selection/sequence/naming can outperform new assets.
- **Evidence adjacent to claims:** context + source placed next to what you assert.
- **Modern credibility > modern style:** clarity + correction culture, not trendy gestures.
- **Silence has function:** negative space increases comprehension/status/attention.
- **Integrity gate:** no avant-garde cosplay; no ethics-free spectacle.

## 3 anti-patterns (avoid)
- **Minimalism as wallpaper:** restraint without craft reads cheap/empty.
- **Reference as decoration:** citations that don’t change meaning/behavior.
- **Avant-garde cosplay:** borrowed “radical” aesthetics without institutional/operational change.
