# Axiom — System Proof (Adoption by Others)

If it can’t be executed by non-designers under deadline across 20+ touchpoints, it’s not a brand system.

## Why it matters
- Protects against “poster design” that dies at the first template.
- Forces governance (tokens, scale, caption rules, spacing) before styling.

## Tests
- Worst-touchpoint test: can the system survive the ugliest real format (email, mobile, tiny label, slide)?
- Can someone else ship 10 assets without inventing new rules?
