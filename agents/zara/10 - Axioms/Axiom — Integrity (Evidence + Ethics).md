# Axiom — Integrity (Evidence + Ethics)

Never use design to lie: claims require adjacent proof; images require ethical context.

## Why it matters
- Builds durable trust (especially in “calm authority” systems).
- Prevents hollow spectacle and aestheticized harm.

## Tests
- Evidence adjacency: is the source/baseline/context next to the claim?
- Ethics gate: are we aestheticizing harm/poverty/trauma for cool?
