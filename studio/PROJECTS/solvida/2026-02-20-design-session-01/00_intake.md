# Project Intake

## What are we making?
Sol Vida round-3 brand system development focused on packaging-first execution with cross-surface consistency (social + landing + pitch surfaces).

## Why does it exist?
Current direction risks default wellness styling. This session forces an ownable system and a single dominant move before aesthetic polishing.

## Who is it for?
Design-aware wellness buyers (roughly 25-42) who want clean, credible, low-friction health products and dislike generic supplement branding.

## What does success look like? (2 weeks / 2 months)
- 2 weeks: one approved system direction with usable rules and six coherent output surfaces.
- 2 months: repeatable launch-ready packaging + content system that scales across SKUs without quality drift.

## Constraints
- Budget: Unknown; assume lean execution (no custom photography in this round).
- Timeline: Fast iteration; round-1 deliverables due in one design cycle.
- Manufacturing/print constraints (if packaging): keep copy hierarchy and contrast print-safe; avoid effects that depend on specialty finishing.
- Tech constraints (if web/app): direction must map cleanly to landing hero + lightweight components.

## Required deliverables
- Idea sentence (one line).
- Three candidate direction frames, one selected.
- 6-surface proof set: IG post, IG story, landing hero, email header, product label, pitch slide.
- Critique with Keep/Kill/Change and explicit next-round tests.
