## Problem Frame
For Sol Vida, the core design challenge is to establish a distinctive visual identity that transcends typical wellness tropes, emphasizing the inherent purity and efficacy of its ingredients through honest, impactful design, while ensuring every element contributes to a clear hierarchy and reflects material truth.

## Evidence / Receipts
- Analysis of competitor packaging in the health & wellness sector showing pervasive "wellness wallpaper" aesthetics.
- Internal brief emphasizing "clarity, hierarchy, and material truth" as foundational design principles for Sol Vida.
- Consumer feedback highlighting desire for authentic and trustworthy brand communication in supplement packaging.

## Opportunity Areas
1. Develop a unique visual language for packaging that leverages structural design and tactile finishes to convey natural purity.
2. Create an intuitive information hierarchy on the label, making key benefits and ingredients immediately scannable and credible.
3. Establish a brand aesthetic that feels both premium and approachable, utilizing textures and forms inspired by nature without being overtly literal.

## Constraints
- Avoid generic wellness wallpaper aesthetics.
- Prioritize clarity, hierarchy, and material truth.
