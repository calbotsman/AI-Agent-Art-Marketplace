## Creative Thesis
To convey Sol Vida's commitment to clarity and material truth through minimalist, functional packaging design that highlights ingredient transparency and efficacy, while avoiding generic wellness aesthetics.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Design must prioritize absolute clarity and legibility of all product information, especially ingredients.
2. The aesthetic must be distinctly modern and minimalist, actively avoiding common "wellness wallpaper" visual tropes.
3. Packaging must adhere strictly to Cyborg label rails and template specifications (1650x600 canvas, 1fr 300px 1fr grid, max 8 ingredient rows).
4. All design elements must reinforce the brand's positioning of premium quality and scientific backing.
5. The final output must include comprehensive validation evidence confirming adherence to all profile constraints.

## Visual System Rules
- Embrace a clean, sans-serif typography hierarchy for maximum readability and a modern feel.
- Utilize a restrained color palette, primarily natural and muted tones, with strategic use of a single accent color for key information.
- Employ ample negative space to create a sense of premium quality and focus attention on essential information.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Copy must be concise, benefit-driven, and scientifically accurate, avoiding jargon or unsubstantiated claims.
- Prioritize clear, direct language that communicates product efficacy and ingredient purity without embellishment.
- Maintain a tone that is authoritative yet approachable, reflecting the brand's transparent and trustworthy nature.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: label.png, label.pdf, product-mock.png, brand-board.png, manifest.json.
- QA gates: canvas must be 1650x600, grid must remain 1fr 300px 1fr, ingredient rows must be <= 8, overflow must be false and collision count must be 0.
- Profile constraints: treat Cyborg label template as read-only reference, do not exceed packaging content rails, all shipped packaging must include validation evidence.

- Minimum reviews before ship: 3
- Target acceptance review: 3
- Maximum reviews: 4

## Handoff to Review
