## Creative Thesis
Authentic, unadulterated ingredient quality expressed through tactile finishes and minimalist forms in packaging design.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand mark and primary product benefit must have immediate, clear hierarchy.
2. Ingredient callouts must be clearly scannable after primary elements.
3. Avoid generic wellness aesthetics (gradient overuse, flowing organic shapes without purpose, muted, indistinguishable color palettes).
4. Packaging design must directly reflect material truth, avoiding artificiality.
5. All elements must communicate transparency and trust through clear information hierarchy.

## Visual System Rules
- Tactile finishes should be explored to convey natural ingredient quality.
- Minimalist forms and layouts are preferred.
- Visual language should be inspired by nature without being literal or generic.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Emphasize purity and efficacy with clear, concise language.
- Communicate transparency and trust through direct, honest phrasing.
- Avoid inflated symbolism or vague attributions.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: label.png, label.pdf, product-mock.png, brand-board.png, manifest.json
- QA gates: canvas must be 1650x600, grid must remain 1fr 300px 1fr, ingredient rows must be <= 8, overflow must be false and collision count must be 0
- Constraints that execution must not break: treat Cyborg label template as read-only reference, do not exceed packaging content rails, all shipped packaging must include validation evidence

## Director Acceptance Timing
- Minimum reviews before ship: 3
- Target acceptance review: 3
- Maximum reviews: 4

## Handoff to Review
- Does the design effectively convey "material truth" and authentic ingredient quality?
- Is the information hierarchy clear, with the brand mark and primary benefit being most prominent?
