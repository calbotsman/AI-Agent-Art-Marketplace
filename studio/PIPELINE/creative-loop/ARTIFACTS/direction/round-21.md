## Creative Thesis
Develop a premium and trustworthy visual identity for Sol Vida that communicates science-backed efficacy, natural purity, and holistic well-being to health-conscious consumers.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Design must convey trust and natural efficacy.
2. Information hierarchy on the label must be clear and intuitive.
3. Design elements must differentiate Sol Vida from generic wellness brands.
4. All designs must adhere to the Cyborg label template specifications.
5. Critical risks from the strategy phase must not be carried forward.

## Visual System Rules
- Use a color palette and typography that evokes natural purity and scientific trustworthiness.
- Employ clean, modern design aesthetics with subtle natural motifs.
- Ensure legibility and scannability of all on-label text, especially ingredient lists.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Copy should be concise, clear, and focused on benefits supported by science.
- Emphasize natural ingredients and transparent sourcing.
- Maintain a premium, authoritative, yet approachable tone.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: label.png, label.pdf, product-mock.png, brand-board.png, manifest.json.
- QA gates: canvas must be 1650x600, grid must remain 1fr 300px 1fr, ingredient rows must be <= 8, overflow must be false and collision count must be 0.
- Profile constraints: treat Cyborg label template as read-only reference, do not exceed packaging content rails, all shipped packaging must include validation evidence.

## Director Acceptance Timing
- Minimum reviews before ship: 3
- Target acceptance review: 3
- Maximum reviews: 4

## Handoff to Review
- Does the design clearly communicate trust and natural efficacy?
- Is the label's information hierarchy intuitive and easy to read?
- Does the overall aesthetic successfully differentiate Sol Vida?
