## Creative Thesis
The creative thesis for this round focuses on achieving a bold yet clean aesthetic that clearly communicates the product's benefits while adhering strictly to the technical specifications of the packaging profile.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand identity elements (logo, primary colors) must be prominent and consistent.
2. Legibility of all text, especially ingredient lists and directions, is paramount.
3. The design must be visually appealing and competitive within the supplement market.
4. Compliance with all specified packaging dimensions and grid structures.
5. All design elements must align with the strategic brief.

## Visual System Rules
- Use a clear, hierarchical typography system for scannability.
- Maintain a balanced layout with adequate white space.
- Employ a color palette that reinforces brand identity and product benefits.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Copy must be concise, benefit-oriented, and easy to understand.
- Prioritize key information and call-to-actions.
- Ensure all claims are accurate and compliant with regulatory guidelines.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: label.png, label.pdf, product-mock.png, brand-board.png, manifest.json

- Required constraints: treat Cyborg label template as read-only reference, do not exceed packaging content rails, all shipped packaging must include validation evidence
