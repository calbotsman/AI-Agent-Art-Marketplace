## Creative Thesis
Sol Vida's packaging will embody premium quality and natural efficacy through a sophisticated, clean aesthetic, ensuring clear communication of benefits and strong brand differentiation.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand mark and core identity elements must be consistently and prominently displayed.
2. All regulatory information and ingredient lists must be clearly legible and comply with packaging standards.
3. The design must convey a sense of natural origin and high-quality ingredients without appearing generic.
4. Color palette and typography must be harmonious and support a premium, trustworthy brand image.
5. The design must be adaptable for potential product line extensions while maintaining core brand recognition.

## Visual System Rules
- Utilize a refined, modern color palette with earthy tones and subtle gradients to convey natural efficacy and premium quality.
- Employ clean, minimalist layouts that prioritize product information and brand elements, avoiding clutter.
- Integrate abstract natural motifs or textures subtly to reinforce the brand's connection to nature.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Employ concise, benefit-driven language that highlights purity, potency, and scientific validation.
- Ensure all claims are clear, accurate, and easily understandable by the target audience.
- Maintain a tone that is authoritative yet accessible, inspiring trust and well-being.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: High-fidelity packaging mockups, print-ready label files, and a comprehensive brand style guide for packaging.
- QA gates: Visual consistency checks, legibility audits for all text, compliance review for regulatory information, and print proofing simulation.
- Profile constraints: Must adhere to physical dimensions of 1650x600 canvas, grid-template-columns: 1fr 300px 1fr, and max 8 ingredient rows.

## Director Acceptance Timing
- Minimum reviews before ship: 3
- Target acceptance review: 3
- Maximum reviews: 4

## Handoff to Review
- Does the design clearly communicate premium quality and natural efficacy?
- Is all critical information legible and well-organized?
- Does the packaging effectively differentiate Sol Vida from competitors?
