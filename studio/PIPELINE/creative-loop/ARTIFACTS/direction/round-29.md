## Creative Thesis
To craft a visually clear and trustworthy packaging design for Sol Vida that communicates natural efficacy and scientific validation, leveraging textures and visual hierarchy to resonate with health-conscious consumers.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Packaging must clearly communicate "natural efficacy" and "scientific validation."
2. Design must be visually compelling, trustworthy, and premium, avoiding generic aesthetics.
3. Label design must comply with regulatory requirements and maintain clear visual hierarchy.
4. Material truths (texture/finish) should be explored to emphasize natural aspects.
5. The overall aesthetic must bridge nature and science with integrity.

## Visual System Rules
- Use clean, modern typography for clarity and readability.
- Incorporate subtle natural textures or gradients to evoke organic origins.
- Employ a balanced layout to ensure key information is easily digestible.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Use concise, benefit-driven language to highlight product advantages.
- Maintain a tone of scientific authority balanced with natural wellness.
- Ensure all claims are supported and presented transparently.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: A high-fidelity packaging label design, a product mock-up, and a brand board.
- QA gates: Visual hierarchy for regulatory compliance, shelf appeal, and consistent brand messaging.
- Profile constraints: 1650x600 canvas, grid-template-columns: 1fr 300px 1fr, max 8 ingredient rows.

## Director Acceptance Timing
- Minimum reviews before ship: 3
- Target acceptance review: 3
- Hard max reviews: 4

## Handoff to Review
- Assess how effectively natural efficacy and scientific validation are communicated.
- Evaluate the clarity and hierarchy of information on the label.
