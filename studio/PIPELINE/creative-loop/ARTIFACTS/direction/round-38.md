## Creative Thesis
A compelling visual system for product packaging that communicates brand values and ensures shelf appeal.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand logo and primary colors must be consistent with established brand guidelines.
2. Legal and regulatory information must be clearly legible and prominent.
3. Design must be adaptable across various product sizes and formats.
4. Packaging must evoke a sense of quality and premium craftsmanship.
5. Must be distinct from competitor packaging.

## Visual System Rules
- Use a clear visual hierarchy to guide the eye to key information.
- Incorporate subtle textures and finishes to enhance tactile experience.
- Maintain a balanced use of white space to prevent visual clutter.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Copy should be concise, benefit-oriented, and easy to understand.
- Tone should be authoritative yet approachable.
- Highlight key ingredients or features without overwhelming the consumer.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: Final print-ready label design (PDF, AI), 3D product mockups (PNG), brand board.
- QA gates: Legal compliance check, print feasibility review, stakeholder approval.
