1. Design must clearly convey purity and natural essence.
2. Packaging must stand out in a crowded supplement market.
3. Design must communicate sustainability and premium quality.
4. Packaging must adhere strictly to Cyborg label rails (1650x600 canvas, 1fr 300px 1fr grid, <= 8 ingredient rows).
5. No single high-momentum logo signal exceeds threshold; explore geometric, typographic, and mark-first variants.
