## Creative Thesis
Develop a visual concept for Sol Vida's packaging that embodies clarity and material truth, focusing on purity, efficacy, and ethical sourcing to appeal to health-conscious consumers.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Packaging must convey a sense of purity and natural ingredients.
2. Design must highlight tangible health benefits clearly.
3. Ethical sourcing and transparency must be communicated through visual elements.
4. The brand identity of Sol Vida (clarity and material truth) must be evident in the design.
5. Packaging must align with the premium positioning of the product.

## Visual System Rules
- Use clean, minimalist aesthetics to emphasize purity.
- Employ natural textures and muted color palettes.
- Ensure clear hierarchy for product name, key benefits, and ingredient information.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Use concise and impactful language for benefits.
- Clearly state ethical sourcing and purity claims.
- Maintain a trustworthy and informative tone.
