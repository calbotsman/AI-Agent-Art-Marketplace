## Creative Thesis
Design Sol Vida packaging that embodies purity and scientific integrity through a modern, clean, and visually compelling aesthetic.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand mark must be prominent and legible.
2. Key product benefits and ingredients must be immediately scannable.
3. Design must convey a sense of natural sourcing and scientific credibility.
4. Adherence to physical packaging dimensions and material considerations.
5. Regulatory information placement must be clear and compliant.

## Visual System Rules
- Use a clean, minimalist layout with ample white space.
- Employ a sophisticated color palette that suggests natural purity and premium quality.
- Utilize modern, sans-serif typography for clarity and a contemporary feel.

## Copy Direction Rules
- Use concise, benefit-driven language.
- Emphasize scientific backing and natural origin without jargon.
- Maintain an encouraging and trustworthy tone.

## Profile Enforcement
- Required deliverables: 3D render, label artwork (PDF/PNG), brand board, manifest.json.
- QA gates: Visual fidelity to Figma, legibility on physical mock-up, compliance with regulatory text size.
- Profile constraints: Must fit 1650x600 canvas, grid-template-columns: 1fr 300px 1fr, max 8 ingredient rows.

## Director Acceptance Timing
- Min reviews: 3, Target acceptance review: 3, Max reviews: 4
