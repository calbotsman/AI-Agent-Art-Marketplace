## Creative Thesis
Design effective supplement packaging that conveys purity, potency, and transparency through a clean, modern aesthetic, aligning with Sol Vida's natural wellness positioning.

## Non-Negotiables
1.  Primary brand elements (logo, name) must be highly legible and prominent.
2.  Ingredient list and supplement facts must be clear, accurate, and easily readable.
3.  Packaging must visually communicate premium quality and natural origin.
4.  All required legal disclaimers and certifications must be present and compliant.
5.  Design must adhere to the 1650x600 canvas and 1fr 300px 1fr grid system.

## Visual System Rules
-   Use a clean, uncluttered layout with ample white space to convey purity.
-   Employ a sophisticated, natural color palette, avoiding overly vibrant or artificial tones.
-   Integrate subtle textures or material finishes that suggest sustainability and organic origins.

## Copy Direction Rules
-   Copy should be concise, benefit-oriented, and reinforce the purity and potency of ingredients.
-   Avoid jargon; use clear, direct language that resonates with health-conscious consumers.
-   Emphasize key differentiators like sourcing transparency and third-party testing.

## Profile Enforcement
-   Required deliverables: label.png, label.pdf, product-mock.png, brand-board.png, manifest.json
-   QA gates: canvas must be 1650x600, grid must remain 1fr 300px 1fr, ingredient rows must be <= 8, overflow must be false and collision count must be 0
-   Constraints: treat Cyborg label template as read-only reference, do not exceed packaging content rails, all shipped packaging must include validation evidence
