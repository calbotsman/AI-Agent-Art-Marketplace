## Creative Thesis
Establish a refined visual language for Sol Vida packaging that balances natural elements with modern precision.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Brand mark should be clearly visible and legible.
2. Product name and key benefits must be prominent.
3. Nutritional information/supplement facts must be accurate and easy to read.
4. Packaging must communicate transparency and quality.
5. Overall design must be elegant and modern.

- Use a clean, minimalist aesthetic with thoughtful use of whitespace.

## Copy Direction Rules

- Emphasize transparency, quality, and efficacy.
- Avoid jargon; use accessible language.
