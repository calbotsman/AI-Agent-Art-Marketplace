## Audience
- Health-conscious consumers seeking transparent product information.
- Individuals valuing authenticity and clear communication in their wellness choices.

## Positioning
Sol Vida offers a refreshing approach to wellness, delivering products with uncompromised clarity and an unwavering commitment to material truth, cutting through market clutter with genuine integrity.

## Message Pillars
1. Unwavering Clarity: Transparent ingredients and straightforward benefits.
2. Authentic Efficacy: Products designed with real results in mind, based on material truth.
3. Elevated Simplicity: A refined aesthetic that communicates purity without being generic.

## Proof Points
- Detailed ingredient sourcing information.
- Scientific backing for key components.
- Positive testimonials from discerning users.

## Strategic Risks
- Risk of being perceived as overly niche or exclusive.
- Challenge of conveying "material truth" without overwhelming scientific jargon.

## Designer Profile Selection
- This round focuses on packaging because it is the primary touchpoint for communicating product truth and aesthetic distinctiveness in a competitive market.
- The packaging profile ensures that clarity, hierarchy, and material truth are physically embodied in the product's presentation, directly addressing the core problem frame.
