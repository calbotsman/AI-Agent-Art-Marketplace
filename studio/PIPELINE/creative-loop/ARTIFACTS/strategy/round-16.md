## Audience
- Health-conscious consumers seeking natural vitality and scientifically-backed supplements.
- Individuals valuing product transparency and effective, high-quality ingredients.

## Positioning
Sol Vida stands as the bridge between nature's potency and scientific precision, offering supplements that deliver tangible results for a vibrant life, encapsulated in design that speaks to both purity and efficacy.

## Message Pillars
1. Natural Vitality: Emphasizing the pure, wholesome origin of ingredients and the energetic benefits.
2. Scientific Efficacy: Highlighting the research-backed formulations and proven results.
3. Transparent Quality: Ensuring clarity on sourcing, ingredients, and manufacturing for consumer trust.

## Proof Points
- Formulated with sustainably sourced, high-potency botanicals.
- Backed by peer-reviewed research for ingredient effectiveness.
- Strict third-party testing for purity and potency.

## Strategic Risks
- Over-complication of messaging trying to balance "natural" and "scientific."
- Visual design failing to clearly differentiate from generic wellness brands.

## Designer Profile Selection
- This round focuses on packaging because it is the primary physical touchpoint and decision-making interface for consumers on-shelf.
- This profile imposes constraints on layout for readability, material considerations for perceived value, and structural integrity for product protection.

## Loop Context
- Minimum reviews before ship: 3, Target acceptance review: 3, Maximum reviews: 4.

## Handoff to Creative Direction
- Translate "natural vitality + scientific precision" into a distinct visual language for packaging.
- Develop clear typographic hierarchy for product name, benefits, and key claims.
- Explore material finishes and structural forms that enhance perceived product quality and brand story.
