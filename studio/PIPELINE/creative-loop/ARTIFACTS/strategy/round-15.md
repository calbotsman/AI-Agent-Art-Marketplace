## Audience
- Health-conscious consumers seeking transparent, high-quality supplements.
- Individuals interested in natural wellness and sustainable product practices.

## Positioning
Sol Vida offers meticulously sourced, effective supplements presented with clarity and material honesty, distinguishing itself from opaque, trend-driven wellness brands.

## Message Pillars
1. Purity and Potency: Emphasize ingredient quality and verifiable efficacy.
2. Transparency and Trust: Highlight clear labeling and ethical sourcing.
3. Natural Harmony: Connect with holistic well-being and environmental respect.

## Proof Points
- Certifications and third-party testing for purity.
- Detailed ingredient origin stories.
- Sustainable packaging materials and practices.

## Strategic Risks
- Market saturation with "natural" and "wellness" brands.
- Communicating scientific rigor without becoming overly clinical.

## Designer Profile Selection
- The packaging profile is correct for this round as the core objective is to translate the brand's commitment to transparency and quality directly onto the physical product, which is the primary touchpoint for initial consumer engagement.
- This profile imposes constraints on visual hierarchy (clear, legible information), material expression (authenticity, sustainability cues), and structural integrity (product protection, user experience).

## Loop Context
- Min reviews: 3, Target acceptance review: 3, Max reviews: 4.

## Handoff to Creative Direction
- Develop visual system that embodies purity, potency, and transparency.
- Establish clear hierarchy for key information on packaging.
- Explore material textures and finishes that convey natural harmony.
