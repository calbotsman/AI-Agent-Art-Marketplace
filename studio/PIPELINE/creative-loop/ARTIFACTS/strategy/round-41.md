## Audience
- Health-conscious consumers seeking natural, high-quality supplements.
- Individuals who value transparent branding and sustainable practices.

## Positioning
Sol Vida offers premium, plant-based nutritional supplements, positioned as a trusted brand for vitality and well-being, emphasizing clarity, material truth, and a strong visual hierarchy on its packaging to convey efficacy and purity.

## Message Pillars
1. Clarity and Purity: Emphasizing clean ingredients and transparent sourcing.
2. Natural Vitality: Highlighting the plant-based origin and energy-boosting benefits.
3. Trust and Efficacy: Building confidence through clear information and reliable quality.

## Proof Points
- Sustainably sourced, plant-based ingredients.
- Rigorous third-party testing for purity.
- Transparent labeling with full ingredient disclosure.

## Strategic Risks
- Overlooking nuanced visual cues that could alienate target demographic.
- Failing to differentiate from crowded wellness market with similar messaging.
